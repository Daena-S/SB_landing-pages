## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which scheduling software has the best free plan?

Setmore and SimplyBook.me both offer permanent free plans. Setmore's free plan supports up to 4 users with unlimited bookings, but lacks SMS reminders, recurring appointments, and payment processing. SimplyBook.me's free plan supports 1 provider and 50 bookings per month with access to one custom feature. Calendly's free plan allows only a single active event type. Acuity Scheduling has no free plan - only a 7-day trial.

### Is SimplyBook.me better than Calendly for service businesses?

For most service businesses, yes. Calendly is built for meeting scheduling and sales workflows, not service businesses managing payments, staff, and client retention. It explicitly refuses HIPAA BAAs, does not support recurring appointments, and offers only Stripe and PayPal for payments. SimplyBook.me supports 40+ payment gateways, HIPAA from the Standard plan, memberships, packages, loyalty programs, and a branded client app - none of which Calendly offers (as of April 2026).

### How does Calendly's per-seat pricing compare to SimplyBook.me?

Calendly charges per seat - a 10-person team on Teams plan costs $160/month annually. SimplyBook.me charges by booking volume, not headcount. The Standard plan at €24.90/month supports up to 15 providers with 500 bookings per month. Adding team members to SimplyBook.me doesn't increase your bill.

### Which scheduling software is HIPAA compliant?

SimplyBook.me includes HIPAA BAA capability on the Standard plan (€24.90/month annual). Acuity requires its Premium plan ($49/month annual). Setmore offers HIPAA compliance via the Setmore Health add-on on Pro or Team plans, though several features are disabled to maintain compliance. Calendly explicitly refuses to sign Business Associate Agreements - making it unsuitable for covered healthcare entities (as of April 2026).

### Does SimplyBook.me support memberships and packages?

Yes. SimplyBook.me supports memberships with recurring billing and multi-session packages on all paid plans. These features are not available on Calendly or Setmore. On Acuity, memberships, packages, and gift certificates require the Standard plan at $27/month or above.

### Which scheduling platform has the most payment gateways?

SimplyBook.me supports 40+ payment gateways including Stripe, PayPal, Square, Mollie, Adyen, Worldpay, Alipay, CCAvenue, and dozens of regional processors. Acuity and Setmore support Stripe, Square, and PayPal only. Calendly supports Stripe and PayPal only. ### What does Acuity Scheduling do better than SimplyBook.me?

Acuity offers unlimited bookings on all plans (SimplyBook.me caps bookings by tier), built-in invoicing, strong email marketing integrations (Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, ConvertKit, and 5 others), and 4 native video conferencing integrations.

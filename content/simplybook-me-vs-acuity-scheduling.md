## Why Businesses Look for an Acuity Scheduling Alternative

If you have been using Acuity Scheduling, you may have run into some of these common limitations:

- **No free plan.** Acuity Scheduling does not offer a free tier. The lowest plan (Starter) costs $16/month billed annually or $20/month billed monthly (as of March 2026, source: acuityscheduling.com/signup.php). You get a 7-day free trial, but after that you pay from day one.

- **Only three payment gateways.** Acuity Scheduling supports Stripe, Square, and PayPal (as of March 2026, source: acuityscheduling.com/integrations). If your business operates in a market where those three do not have strong coverage, or you need a regional gateway like Mollie, Adyen, or Alipay, you will need a workaround.

- **HIPAA compliance is expensive.** Acuity requires the Premium plan ($39/month annual, $49/month monthly) to sign a BAA for HIPAA compliance (as of March 2026, source: acuityscheduling.com/signup.php). For healthcare providers on a budget, that is a steep entry point for a basic compliance requirement.

- **Key features locked behind higher plans.** SMS reminders, packages, memberships, and gift certificates all require the Standard plan at $27/month or above (as of March 2026, source: acuityscheduling.com/signup.php). On the Starter plan, you get email reminders only.

SimplyBook.me addresses each of these gaps with a free plan, 40+ payment gateways, and HIPAA compliance starting on the Standard plan at €24.90/month (billed annually).

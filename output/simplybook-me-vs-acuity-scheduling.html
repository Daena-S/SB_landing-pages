Why Businesses Look for an Acuity Scheduling Alternative

If you have been using Acuity Scheduling, you may have run into some of these common limitations:

  • No free plan. Acuity Scheduling does not offer a free tier. The lowest plan (Starter) costs $16/month billed annually or $20/month billed monthly. You get a 7-day free trial, but after that you pay from day one.
  • Only three payment gateways. Acuity supports Stripe, Square, and PayPal. If your business operates in a market where those three do not have strong coverage, or you need a regional gateway like Mollie, Adyen, or Alipay, you will need a workaround.
  • HIPAA compliance is expensive. Acuity requires the Premium plan ($39/month annual, $49/month monthly) to sign a BAA for HIPAA compliance. For healthcare providers on a budget, that is a steep entry point for a basic compliance requirement.
  • Key features locked behind higher plans. SMS reminders, packages, memberships, and gift certificates all require the Standard plan at $27/month or above. On the Starter plan, you get email reminders only.

SimplyBook.me addresses each of these gaps with a free plan, 40+ payment gateways, and HIPAA compliance starting on the Standard plan at €24.90/month (billed annually).

Consider a salon or clinic with 6 staff members. On Acuity, you need the Standard plan at $27/month (annual) to get 6 calendars. On SimplyBook.me, the Basic plan at €11.90/month (annual) supports up to 5 providers, and the Standard plan at €24.90/month supports up to 15.

Frequently Asked Questions About SimplyBook.me vs Acuity Scheduling

Is SimplyBook.me better than Acuity Scheduling for small businesses?

It depends on your needs. SimplyBook.me offers a permanent free plan, 40+ payment gateways, and features like loyalty programs, waiting lists, and a branded client app that Acuity does not list (as of March 2026). Acuity offers unlimited bookings on all plans and stronger email marketing integrations through Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, and others. For businesses that need payment flexibility or a free starting point, SimplyBook.me is the stronger fit.

How much does SimplyBook.me cost compared to Acuity Scheduling?

SimplyBook.me starts free (1 provider, 50 bookings/month). Paid plans range from €11.90 to €49.90/month (billed annually). Acuity Scheduling starts at $16/month (annual) with no free plan. The key difference is pricing structure: SimplyBook.me charges by booking volume, Acuity charges by the number of calendars (as of March 2026).

Does Acuity Scheduling have a free plan?

No. Acuity Scheduling offers a 7-day free trial but no permanent free plan (as of March 2026). SimplyBook.me offers a free plan with 1 provider and 50 bookings per month, with no time limit.

Yes. Export your client list from Acuity as a CSV file and import it directly into SimplyBook.me using the client import tool. Services, staff schedules, and business hours can be set up through the setup wizard in under 15 minutes.

Is SimplyBook.me HIPAA compliant? How does it compare to Acuity for healthcare?

SimplyBook.me is HIPAA compliant with BAA capability on the Standard plan (€24.90/month annual). It also includes encrypted SOAP notes and SAML SSO. Acuity Scheduling requires the Premium plan ($39/month annual) for HIPAA BAA, and does not list SOAP notes or SSO (as of March 2026).

What payment gateways does SimplyBook.me support that Acuity does not?

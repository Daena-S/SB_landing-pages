The Real Cost of Manual Booking

If you still rely on phone calls, DMs, and spreadsheets to manage appointments, you already know the pain. But the cost goes deeper than wasted time.

  • Phone tag kills bookings. A potential client calls while you are with someone. They do not leave a voicemail. They book with someone else. You never even know you lost that revenue.
  • Double bookings destroy trust. Two clients show up for the same slot. One of them leaves frustrated, and you spend the rest of the day apologizing instead of working.
  • No-shows drain your income. When booking is too easy to forget, clients forget. The average service business loses 10-15% of revenue to no-shows every month.
  • Admin overhead steals your evenings. Instead of closing up and going home, you are confirming tomorrow's appointments by hand, updating a spreadsheet, and chasing late payments.

There is a better way. And it does not require a receptionist, a developer, or a six-month implementation.

Frequently Asked Questions About Online Booking With SimplyBook.me

What is SimplyBook.me and how does it work?

SimplyBook.me is an online booking and scheduling platform that lets your clients book appointments through your website, social media, or a dedicated booking page. You set your services, availability, and business rules. Clients see open slots in real time and book instantly. You receive automatic notifications and your calendar updates across all channels.

How much does SimplyBook.me cost?

SimplyBook.me offers a free plan for businesses with basic scheduling needs. Paid plans start at $9.90 per month and scale based on the number of bookings and features you need. Every paid plan includes a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. Full pricing details are available at simplybook.me/en/pricing.

Can SimplyBook.me help reduce no-shows?

Yes. SimplyBook.me sends automated email and SMS reminders at intervals you choose. Businesses using these reminders report up to 30% fewer no-shows. You can also require deposits or full prepayment at booking, which further reduces missed appointments.

Do I need technical skills to set up SimplyBook.me?

No. The setup wizard guides you through creating your booking page, adding services, and configuring your availability. Most businesses go live in under 30 minutes. If you need help, live chat support is available directly from your dashboard.

Can I accept payments through SimplyBook.me?

Yes. SimplyBook.me integrates with Stripe, PayPal, Square, and over 10 additional payment gateways. You can collect full payments, deposits, or sell packages and gift cards directly through your booking page. Funds go straight to your connected account.

Is SimplyBook.me suitable for businesses with multiple locations?

Absolutely. SimplyBook.me supports multi-location management from a single dashboard. Each location can have its own services, staff, and availability while you maintain centralized reporting and control. Over 100,000 businesses worldwide use the platform, including multi-branch operations.

Does SimplyBook.me integrate with my existing calendar?

Yes. SimplyBook.me syncs with Google Calendar, Outlook, and Apple Calendar. Bookings made through SimplyBook.me appear in your personal calendar automatically, and your personal events block availability on your booking page so clients never double-book you.

What industries use SimplyBook.me for appointment scheduling?

SimplyBook.me serves health and wellness providers, beauty salons, fitness studios, education centers, professional services firms, and government agencies, among others. The platform supports HIPAA compliance for US healthcare, GDPR for EU businesses, and ISO 27001 certified security. With 50+ custom features, it adapts to the specific workflows of virtually any service-based industry.

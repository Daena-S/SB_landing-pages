## 1. Competitor Overview

- **Company name:** Calendly
- **Website:** https://calendly.com
- **What they do:** Calendly is a scheduling automation platform designed to eliminate the back-and-forth of arranging meetings. It works with calendars and customizes schedules based on availability preferences and time-zone detection. (source: https://calendly.com/about, verified 2026-04-06)
- **Target audience:** Sales teams, recruiters, customer success teams, revenue organizations, and individual professionals who need to schedule external meetings. Calendly markets heavily to B2B sales and enterprise teams. (source: https://calendly.com, verified 2026-04-06)
- **Founded:** 2013, in Atlanta, by Tope Awotona (source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Calendly, verified 2026-04-06)
- **Notable context:** Valued at $3 billion as of January 2021. Reported $276.1 million revenue by end of 2023. Used by 86% of Fortune 500 companies as of June 2024. Operates as an all-remote workforce. (source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Calendly, verified 2026-04-06)

## 7. Flags and Gaps

### Unverified Claims
- Calendly's exact feature list per plan could not be fully extracted from calendly.com/pricing directly (page rendered as JS/CSS on fetch). Feature-per-plan details were corroborated via Calendly's help center and multiple third-party pricing guides. The data is consistent across sources but ideally should be spot-checked against the live pricing page.
- Calendly's "meeting packages" feature was found referenced in blog posts but not prominently on the main features or pricing page. Verify this is currently available and on which plans.
- SimplyBook.me's SOC 2 status could not be confirmed. Their security page does not mention it.
- SimplyBook.me's PCI compliance status is not explicitly stated on their security page, though they process payments through PCI-compliant gateways.

### Stale Data Warnings
- Calendly's Wikipedia page references employee count (424) from April 2022 and valuation ($3B) from January 2021. Current figures may differ.
- The claim about "86% of Fortune 500 companies" using Calendly is from June 2024 and may have changed.

### Contradictions
- G2 review star distribution for Calendly shows "84% five-star, 12% four-star, 1% three-star, 0% two-star or one-star" which does not add up to 100%. The remaining percentage may represent unrated reviews or a rounding artifact. The 4.7/5 aggregate score is consistent across G2 and Capterra.
- Calendly's Trustpilot score (2.9/5) contrasts sharply with G2 (4.7/5) and Capterra (4.7/5). This is likely because Trustpilot captures more consumer/billing complaints while G2/Capterra focus on professional software evaluation. Both data points are valid but serve different narratives.

### SimplyBook.me Data Notes
- SimplyBook.me pricing is listed in EUR on the pricing page. Previous brand materials may reference USD pricing. The landing page writer should confirm which currency to display.
- The brand-approved figure of "12,000 businesses in 100+ countries" was not re-verified in this research session. The landing page writer should check the current homepage for the latest figure.
- SimplyBook.me has 118 reviews on G2 compared to Calendly's 2,615. This volume difference should be considered when presenting review comparisons -- the scores are valid but the sample sizes are very different.

## 8. Research Summary

- **Total sourced claims:** ~95+ individual facts with source URLs and verification dates
- **Unverified / flagged claims:** 6 (Calendly meeting packages plan availability, SimplyBook.me SOC 2 status, SimplyBook.me PCI status, Calendly employee count currency, "12,000 businesses" figure, Calendly pricing page direct verification)
- **Recommendation:** Yes, with caveats. The data is sufficient to write a legally compliant comparison page. The key differentiators are clear and well-sourced: SimplyBook.me offers HIPAA compliance, POS, memberships, gift cards, loyalty programs, recurring appointments, white-label, and a branded client app -- none of which Calendly provides. Calendly's strengths are in CRM/sales routing (Salesforce, HubSpot), video conferencing integrations, and its strong brand recognition in the B2B meeting-scheduling space. Before publishing, manually verify: (1) Calendly's current pricing on their live page, (2) SimplyBook.me's "12,000 businesses" claim on the homepage, and (3) whether Calendly's meeting packages feature is currently active and on which plans.

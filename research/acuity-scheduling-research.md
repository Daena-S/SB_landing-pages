## 7. Flags and Gaps

### Unverified Claims
- **Acuity G2 rating discrepancy:** Previous research (2026-03-27) found 4.1/5 via search snippet. Current research (2026-04-06) found 4.6/5 via a different search snippet. G2 returned 403 on direct fetch both times. The 4.6/5 figure came from a more specific search and may be more accurate. Recommend manually checking G2 before publication.
- **Acuity "Client Loyalty" feature scope:** Listed on features page as including recurring appointments, subscriptions, gift certificates, discount codes, and add-on upselling. This appears to be a category of features rather than a points-based loyalty system like SimplyBook.me's. Cannot confirm definitively whether Acuity has a loyalty points system.
- **SimplyBook.me Zapier integration:** Not listed on the features page. May be available but could not confirm from primary source. The SimplyBook.me website had SSL certificate issues on 2026-04-06 which prevented direct verification.
- **SimplyBook.me video conferencing:** Zoom is mentioned in third-party search results but Gruveo is the only video platform explicitly listed on the features page. Could not confirm Zoom integration from primary source.

### Stale Data Warnings
- Acuity's pricing page (signup.php) does not display a "last updated" date. Pricing figures confirmed as consistent with third-party sources reporting in 2026.
- SimplyBook.me website had an SSL certificate issue on 2026-04-06. Pricing and feature data corroborated via Capterra, G2, and third-party review sites but direct verification was limited.

### Contradictions
- **Acuity plan naming:** Third-party sources (G2, Capterra, review sites) still reference "Emerging, Growing, Powerhouse, Enterprise" plan names while the live Acuity signup page uses "Starter, Standard, Premium." The pricing amounts match across both naming conventions. The signup page is the authoritative source.
- **Acuity Premium monthly price:** The signup page appears to show $49/mo for Premium with $61/mo listed in a different context. Some third-party sources list $61/mo as the monthly price and $49/mo as the annual price. The signup page text says "Monthly: $61/mo | Annual: $49/mo" based on our fetch. Previous research listed $49/mo monthly and $39/mo annual. This needs manual verification.
- **Acuity G2 rating:** Previous research found 4.1/5, current research found 4.6/5. Both from search snippets since G2 blocks direct access.
